Liberty High School students briefly evacuated after bomb threat

Bethlehem officials said Liberty High School students have been evacuated after a bomb threat was found in the bathroom.
Bethlehem officials said Liberty High School students have been evacuated after a bomb threat was found in the bathroom. (FILE PHOTO / THE MORNING CALL)
Pamela LehmanContact ReporterOf The Morning Call

Students at Liberty High School in Bethlehem were briefly evacuated Friday after a bomb threat was discovered, according to the superintendent.

Around 9:15 a.m., Bethlehem Area School District Superintendent Joseph Roy tweeted that Liberty students have been evacuated to the stadium.

Roy said the bomb threat was written in a restroom.

By 10:30 a.m., Roy said students had returned to class and the “investigation identify perpetrator of the hoax is well underway.”
